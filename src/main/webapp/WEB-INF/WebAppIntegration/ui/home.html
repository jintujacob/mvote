Election Management Dashboard

Election Operations

Elections Management functions includes mainly four operations. They are Election management, consitituency management, candidate management, Elections Results fetching. Each of this functionalities have it's sub functions.

Voter Enrollment operations

VoterList Management functions cover searching of the voter, Verification of the voters information in the voter list and Adhaar database, Resetting of the password of the enrolled voter etc. Voter Enrollment process is done by manual verification of the originals submitted by the voter.
