E- Election Portal

India, also known as Bharat, is a Union of States. It is a Sovereign Socialist Secular Democratic Republic with a parliamentary system of government. India has an asymmetric federal government, with elected officials at the federal, state and local levels.The Republic is governed in terms of the Constitution of India which was adopted by the Constituent Assembly on 26th November, 1949 and came into force on 26th January, 1950. Voting and elections are essential ingredient of indian democratic society. The purpose of OVS (Online Voting System) is to elect candidate through online voting and the results can be announced instantly. With the OVS, a voter can use his\her voting right online without any difficulty. In this system people who have citizenship of India and whose age is above 18 years of age and any sex can give his\her vote online without going to any physical polling station.
